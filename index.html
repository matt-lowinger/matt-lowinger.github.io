HemaComply EMAC — Implementation Lead

Implementation Lead for the rollout of HemaComply EMAC — a healthcare compliance application managing preventive maintenance and quality control for lab equipment across NIH blood centers. Led product requirements gathering, configured the application, and implemented real-time alerts and detailed reporting features enabling lab supervisors to create customized maintenance schedules by asset tag or serial number.

Jun 2020 — Jan 2021 Allegis Group

LifeVue — Talent Intelligence Platform

Product Manager and Product Owner for LifeVue, a talent platform for Allegis Group — a $13B+ global leader in talent solutions. Led the initiative from conception to launch: defined product features balancing stakeholder needs with market research, built product backlogs and roadmaps, facilitated communication between client and engineering, and integrated user research feedback into every iteration. Launched with a freemium monetization model.

Area of Impact

Increased user engagement and bridged the gap between professional and personal branding — delivering richer, more complete candidate profiles and opening new talent acquisition opportunities.

Mar 2020 — Feb 2021 MedStar Health

OCT Imaging Mobile Application

Founding Product Manager for MedStar Health ($6B+ organization), building a specialized mobile app to improve treatment of in-stent restenosis using Optical Coherence Tomography imaging. Developed the full product vision and roadmap tailored to three distinct user groups — cardiologists, trainees, and cath lab technologists — with features for ISR classification display, image favoriting, and case sharing.

Area of Impact

Achieved high clinical adoption rates, improved treatment decision-making for cardiologists, and fostered community engagement through shared imaging and insights across the medical community.
